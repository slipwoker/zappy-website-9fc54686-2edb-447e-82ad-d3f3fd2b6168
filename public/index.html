Our Process

Made with Patience,
Finished with Precision.

Every piece is shaped through disciplined detailing, thoughtful material selection, and an insistence on longevity. Like the finest custom cabinet makers, we believe that true luxury is felt long after the first impression.

Material Selection

We source only the finest solid woods — white oak, walnut, and aged teak — alongside natural stone, full-grain leather, and premium textiles. Each material is chosen for its character, durability, and the way it matures gracefully with time.

Tailored Detailing

Proportion is everything. From the tension of a hand-stitched seam to the precision of mortise-and-tenon joinery, every detail is considered. Our artisans refine each finish by hand — sanding, oiling, and inspecting until the surface speaks for itself.

Lasting Comfort

GTIM GROUP furniture is designed to age beautifully in real interiors. Structures are engineered for decades of use, cushions built with layered density foam and down wrapping, and surfaces treated to develop a living patina that only improves over time.
